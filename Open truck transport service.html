Open Truck & Lorry Transport Services

An Eicher 19 feet or Eicher 20 feet open truck (Eicher body) is a type of commercial vehicle characterized by its open cargo space. These trucks are commonly used for transporting goods such as industrial equipment and building supplies, making them ideal for various logistics and delivery applications. Their design allows for easy loading and unloading, accommodating a range of cargo sizes and types.

10 Wheeler Truck (10 Tyre Truck / 10 Chakka Truck)

A 10 wheeler truck, also known as a 10 tyre truck or 10 chakka truck are large commercial vehicles that have six wheels, with four of them being driving wheels. They offer significant advantages for businesses that need to transport large quantities of goods over short distances, say Chennai to Trichy. With over 1500 verified 10 wheel lorry, we stand as a reliable partner for all your logistics needs.

Ideal for: Medium-sized cargo loads, palletized goods, machinery transportation.

Service Coverage: Our 10 wheeler trucks are used for transporting goods and machinery within a city or surrounding regions. Our services extend nationwide.

Goods we transport: We transport all kinds of goods that will fit on 10 wheel/10 tyre/10 chakka trucks, like steel and metal products, construction materials, pipes, machinery, agricultural equipment, and more.

12 Wheeler Truck (12 Tyre Truck / 12 Chakka Truck)

A 12-wheeler truck is a versatile vehicle commonly used for transporting goods over medium to long distances. They are not too big, not too small, but just right. These trucks are used to ensure prompt and efficient delivery of goods to customers.

TIdeal for: Oversized or overweight cargo, construction materials, and industrial equipment.

Service Coverage: 12-tyre trucks are perfect for long-distance hauls, providing services across the country.

Goods we transport: We transport all kinds of goods that will fit on 12-wheel/12-tyre/12-chakka trucks, including construction materials, machinery, industrial equipment such as generators, oversized project cargo, bulk commodities like coal and grains, and more.
