FAQ's

General Questions

What transportation services does our offer?
A transportation service provider specializing in open trucks and container trucks offers efficient goods transport across various regions. These services are tailored to meet the needs of different industries, including long-haul transportation, intra-city deliveries, and on-time tracking. With a technology-driven platform, such providers ensure transparency, real-time GPS tracking, cost-effective pricing, and flexibility for both shippers and truck owners, optimizing logistics and supply chain operations.
What are the advantages of choosing an online truck booking service?
The major advantages of choosing an online truck booking service are:

1. Verified Trucks & No Brokers: Only certified trucks are used, eliminating the risks associated with unreliable brokers and ensuring a dependable service.

2. Performance Monitoring: Truck performance is continuously monitored, and underperforming trucks (those averaging below 350 km/day) are removed, ensuring faster and more efficient deliveries.

3. Real-Time Tracking: All trucks are equipped with GPS tracking, allowing you to monitor your shipment’s progress in real-time and stay updated on delivery status.

4. Access to Documents: Customers have easy access to important documents such as proof of delivery, loading memos, and truck-related documents like TDS, PAN, etc., enhancing transparency and ease of operations.
How cost-effective is hiring a truck through our service?
Hiring a truck with our online booking service is highly cost-effective due to several factors. Our app connects your shipment to nearby trucks and runs a competitive bidding process to secure the best price. Transparent pricing allows direct communication with truck owners, helping to avoid hidden charges. Additionally, our service offers trucks across India, enabling centralized procurement that reduces overall hiring costs and facilitates efficient goods movement. This combination of features ensures you can optimize your transportation costs while receiving reliable service.
What are the payment options for online truck bookings?
Two payment options are available for booking trucks:

90% Advance Payment: Pay 90% of the total cost when the truck is loaded, with the remaining 10% due after receiving the Proof of Delivery (POD).

100% Payment on Delivery: Pay the full amount (100%) upon delivery of the goods.

How are truck rates determined?
Truck hire charges are based on the spot market, influenced by several factors. The primary considerations include the distance between the source and destination as well as the availability of trucks at the time of booking. Our online truck booking app uses a matching algorithm to connect your load with the nearest available truck and initiates a bidding process. The best truck, with the most competitive bid, is then assigned to your load, ensuring optimal pricing for your transportation needs.
How to book trucks online with our service?
Booking trucks online is easy and can be done in just three simple steps:

1. Create an Indent: Provide your pickup and delivery locations, cargo details, and preferred dates.

2. Get Quotes: Our platform will match your requirements with available trucks and generate competitive quotes instantly.

3. Book Your Truck: Select the best option based on price, truck details, and driver information.

Do you provide real-time tracking for transportation services?
Yes, we offer real-time tracking for your transportation services. You can instantly track your truck using our online truck booking app to check the status of your goods at any time.
Is our online truck booking app complex?
No, our online truck booking app is designed to be simple and convenient. It features user-friendly interfaces, secure and rapid payment options, and innovative bidding systems that make truck booking easy and efficient for your transportation needs. The process is straightforward: you simply create an indent, check truck rates, and select your desired truck.
What happens if my shipment weight or dimensions differ from what I booked online?
If your shipment weight or dimensions differ from what you booked online, please inform us immediately. We will assess the situation and provide guidance on potential adjustments to your booking or any additional charges that may apply.
How many branches do you have in India?
We provide transportation services across India and have a single physical branch.
